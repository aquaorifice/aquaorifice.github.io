<h3 class="section-heading">Conference Papers</h3>

<div class="publication">
    <div class="pub-title">Exploring Wavelet Trees as Space-Efficient Physical-to-Sorted Mapping for Learned Indexes</div>
    <div class="pub-authors">A. Saha, A. Raman, R. Marcus, M. Athanassoulis</div>
    <div class="pub-venue">AIDB @ VLDB 2025 - Workshop on Applied AI for Database Systems</div>
    <div class="pub-links">
        <a href="https://disc.bu.edu/papers/aidb25-saha">[PDF]</a>
        <a href="https://github.com/BU-DiSC/wavelet-tree-mapping">[Code]</a>
    </div>
    <p style="margin-top: 10px;">
        We explore wavelet trees as a space-efficient alternative for physical-to-sorted mapping in learned indexes, demonstrating how traditional data structures can be evolved to diverse applications and evaluate the space-performance tradeoff observed in systems.
    </p>
</div>

<div class="publication">
    <div class="pub-title">AXE: A Task Decomposition Approach to Learned LSM Tuning</div>
    <div class="pub-authors">A. Huynh, A. Saha, H. Chaudhari, M. Athanassoulis</div>
    <div class="pub-venue">PVLDB (to appear)</div>
    <div class="pub-links">
        <a href="https://disc.bu.edu/papers/vldb25-huynh">[PDF]</a>
        <a href="https://github.com/BU-DiSC/proj_axe">[Code]</a>
    </div>
    <p style="margin-top: 10px;">
        A task decomposition approach to learned LSM tuning that breaks down the complex tuning problem into two sub-tasks - a cost model used that evaluates a given tuning and a tuner that suggests knob values based on the cost model.
    </p>
</div>
